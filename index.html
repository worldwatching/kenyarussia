Wall of Caution

Introduction

As Russian influence takes a chokehold over many countries on the African continent, Kenya should be cognizant of the people within its borders who have real but quiet ties with the war-making nation with a view to the future peace, prosperity, and independence of the East African nation.

Huku ushawishi wa Urusi ukizidi kushika kasi katika nchi nyingi za bara la Afrika, Kenya inapaswa kuwatambua watu walio ndani ya mipaka yake ambao wana uhusiano wa kweli lakini tulivu na taifa hilo linalofanya vita kwa nia ya amani, ustawi na uhuru wa siku zijazo. Taifa la Afrika Mashariki.

Religion

The Orthodox Church of Kenya has been affected by the Russian invasion of Ukraine. Reports of bribery (hefty salaries and other benefits) to break away from the Greek Orthodox Church have resulted in the creation of Russian Orthodox Churches and mass baptisms concentrated in Western Kenya.

Dini

Kanisa la Orthodox la Kenya limeathiriwa na uvamizi wa Urusi nchini Ukraine. Ripoti za hongo (mishahara mikubwa na marupurupu mengine) ya kujitenga na Kanisa Othodoksi la Ugiriki zimesababisha kuundwa kwa Makanisa ya Kiorthodoksi ya Urusi na ubatizo wa watu wengi uliojikita Magharibi mwa Kenya.
